SCREAMLINE

Sometimes, you just need to scream. No judgment. No response. Just release.

CALL NOW
What is SCREAMLINE?
Not a hotline. Not therapy. Not someone waiting to fix you.

SCREAMLINE is an anonymous scream space.
No greetings. No advice. No voices. Just an open line to nowhere — and everywhere.
“You call. You scream. You disappear. You survive.”
Why Do People Use It?
Screamed after a breakup. Fell asleep like a baby.
2AM. Car. Full volume. Felt human again.
Didn’t know why. Didn’t need to.
Not poetic. Not beautiful. Just necessary.
“I screamed. That’s the sentence.”
How It Works
01. Call the number. Don’t think. Just dial.
02. Let it out. Scream. Cry. Sob. Whisper.
03. Hang up. Like nothing happened. Like everything changed.
04. Walk away lighter.
05. Repeat when silence gets too loud.
Is It a Joke?
Yes. And no. It’s satire. It’s therapy. It’s art.
It’s rebellion in disguise. Not a punchline. A pressure valve.
The world wants your whisper. SCREAMLINE gives you the mic.
“What if your soul didn’t need to be healed... What if it just needed to be heard?”

You don’t always need to explain.
You don’t need to hold it together.
You don’t need to be strong today.

“Sometimes, you just need to scream into the night,
Feel your breath bounce back,
and realize —
You're still here. You're real.”

Even silence screams.

(Try again when you need it most)

SCREAMLINE is not a real service — but the need it represents is real.

This experimental project explores voice, release, and invisible breakdowns.
You are not alone in the noise. Even if no one hears it — scream anyway.
